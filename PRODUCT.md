product

## Users
- **Patients**: Stressed or anxious individuals seeking clear, calm, and reassuring access to their medical data, history, and appointments. They require high readability, intuitive layouts, and supportive wording.
- **Lab & Clinic Staff**: Busy healthcare professionals entering and verifying results, needing high ergonomics, clear status indicators, and reliable data-entry patterns.

## Product Purpose
MediTrack is a patient-centric and laboratory-facing healthcare management platform. It bridges the gap between lab diagnostics and patients by providing a secure, clear, and reassuring way to book appointments, view and understand test results, and find nearby pharmacies. Success means reducing patient interpretation anxiety while maximizing lab operator workflow efficiency.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Professional, expert, and clinical yet gentle, empathetic, and reassuring.
- **Personality**: Calm, Trustworthy, Intentional.
- **Emotional Goals**: Instill clarity, peace of mind, security, and absolute confidence in patients, while providing clinic staff with a highly reliable and focused tool.

## Anti-references
- **AI/SaaS Dashboard Clichés**: Overloaded layouts with excessive cards, generic side-stripe borders, and arbitrary hero-metric grids.
- **Legacy Medical Portals**: Clunky, intimidating legacy software with unaligned inputs and dense, confusing tables.
- **Aggressive Dark/Gaming Themes**: Avoid heavy, pure-black dark modes, neon highlights, and overly playful/bouncy animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Calm Clarity Over Complexity**: Visual density must serve a purpose. Highlight normal ranges clearly, and use plenty of whitespace so crucial medical data is easy to parse.
2. **Dual-User Ergonomics**: Ensure patient-facing views feel warm, accessible, and supportive, while clinic-facing views are structured, highly readable, and frictionless.
3. **Inclusive by Design**: High text contrast, robust typography, and minimal decorative motion, creating an accessible environment for users of all ages and cognitive/physical abilities.
4. **Frictionless Navigation**: Make workflows like booking appointments or inputting clinical notes logical and structured, minimizing cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG Level**: WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance.
- **Considerations**: Excellent contrast ratios, robust support for text scaling, clear keyboard focus, and low-stimulus animations for patients navigating under stress or cognitive strain.
